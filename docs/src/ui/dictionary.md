# Dictionary

Look up word definitions while you read. Dictionaries are stored on your device
and work without an internet connection once downloaded.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Download a dictionary first — see
> [Dictionaries](settings/dictionaries.md) for how to install one.

## Looking Up Words While Reading

### Long press on a word

1. Tap and hold a word until it highlights.
2. Keep holding a moment longer until the dictionary opens with that word.

### From a text selection

1. Tap and hold a word want to define.
2. When the word highlights, a menu opens
3. Tap **Define**.

## Opening the Dictionary App

You can also open the dictionary on its own:

## Main Menu → Applications → Dictionary

The search field is focused automatically so you can type a word right away.

## Searching for a Word

1. Tap the search bar at the top of the screen.
2. Type the word you want to look up.
3. Press **Return**.

To type accented characters, see the [Keyboard](keyboard.md) guide.

## Reading Results

Definitions may span more than one screen. To move between pages:

- Swipe left or right, or
- Tap the arrow icons in the bottom bar, or
- Tap the left or right half of the definition area.

To look up another word from a definition:

- Tap a cross-reference link, or
- Long-press a word in the definition text.

## Choosing Which Dictionary to Search

Tap the label in the bottom bar to pick a specific dictionary or **All**.

When you open the dictionary from the reader, Cadmus may already pick a
dictionary that matches the book's language.

## Fuzzy Search

If you are not sure how a word is spelled:

1. Tap the search icon.
2. Toggle **Fuzzy**.

Cadmus will also match words that differ by one letter.
